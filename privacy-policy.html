Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Information We Collect

CyberTools is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics to improve our tools
  • Error Logs: Technical error information (with IP addresses anonymized)
  • Tool Input Data: Data you enter into our tools is processed client-side when possible and not stored

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Provide and improve our cybersecurity tools
  • Monitor system performance and uptime
  • Debug technical issues
  • Ensure service security and prevent abuse

3. Data Storage and Security

Your privacy is paramount:

  • Most tools process data client-side in your browser
  • No personal data is permanently stored unless explicitly stated
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • We follow industry-standard security practices

4. Usage Limits

Free Usage Policy:

  • 10 scans per tool per day for anonymous users
  • Rate limiting may apply to prevent abuse
  • Fair usage policy applies to all tools

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and tracking:

  • Essential cookies for tool functionality
  • Privacy-conscious analytics (anonymized)
  • No third-party advertising cookies
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Report privacy concerns

7. Compliance

We comply with applicable privacy regulations:

  • GDPR: EU General Data Protection Regulation
  • CCPA: California Consumer Privacy Act
  • Other regional privacy laws as applicable
